Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including the following:

  • Microphone Access with Recording
    • To record users' voice inputs in order to search for restaurants
    • After recording and processing the input through speech to text on the users' devices, we do not store the information any further.
  • Camera Access
    • To take and upload photos of menus from restaurants
    • Users' photos are transmitted to the Google Optical Character Recognition API to be converted to a accessible format. We do not store these information any further. Access Google LLC's privacy policy below for more information.
  • Photo Access
    • To choose and upload photos of menus from restaurants
    • Users' photos are transmitted to the Google Optical Character Recognition API to be converted to a accessible format. We do not store these information any further. Access Google LLC's privacy policy below for more information.
  • Non-Continuous Location Access
    • To search restaurants in the users' proximity
    • Users' location are only retrieved when the app is initially opened. We transmit this information to the Google Places API to retrieve restaurants in the users' proximity. We do not store these information any further. Access Google LLC's privacy policy below for more information.

The information that we request is will be retained by us while we transmit necessary information and used as described above.

Users are free to refuse any of these information collection and usage when prompted, but may be restricted from various functionalities of our application.

We use various Google services, such as Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Google Cloud Products such as Places API, Natural Language Processing, and Optical Character Recognition. The Privacy Policy for these services provided by Google LLC can be found here.
